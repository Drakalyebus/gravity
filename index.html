Gravity simulation

Click and drag to move the camera.

Left click to add an object.

Right click to add an object to the watch list.

Scroll to zoom in/out.

To open the settings, hover over the tab on the left side of the screen.

When the "Objects preview" mode is enabled, all added objects will be displayed in the upper left corner of the screen, and the white object in the "Local time" mode will show the global time of the observer.

This simulation is based on both Newton's laws and Einstein's theory of relativity, and objects have a clock hand visible on them that shows how the local time of that object moves.
